Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide door to door service?

YES, we provide door-to-door service. We will make every attempt to accommodate our valued customers. If accessibility is not possible, our drivers will arrange a reasonable alternate location.

How much is your Service charge?

Service charges are different depending on the quantity and expected time of service.
For a rough estimate, please use our estimate calculator on this website. For an exact quote, please contact us here.

How long does it take to deliver one shipment?

If you have urgent cargo, let us know immediately and we will plan for it.
Keep in mind that getting your cargo onto the first-available sail date and vessel may sometimes mean higher prices.

Can you give us credit for the payment?

YES, we will!
You are welcome to use any of your credit for future payments as well.

How extensive is your network?

We have a reliable and established international network which helps us facilitate the shipping process for our clients for a more streamlined end-to-end process.
